Project Components

OREC-SAHEL is a Non-profit, non-political and non-denominational non-governmental organization created on January 23, 2021 on the initiative of a few people mobilized and committed to contribute to strengthening the resilience capacities of local populations to ensure food security and improve access to basic social services for sustainable development, through the five (5) components of the organization !

Component 1

strengthen (i) climate-smart agriculture to combat the adverse effects of climate change (ii) develop agropastoral value chains, promote income-generating activities in rural areas (iii) and data collection and dissemination

Component 2

focus on health nutrition, sexual and reproductive health rights including medico-social assistance to people in emergency situations

Component 3

Strengthen girls' education and advocacy, Literacy, vocational training and local entrepreneurship for youth and women

Component 4

Reduce the number of cases of gender-based violence, strengthen the promotion of a culture of peace and social cohesion within communities

Component 5

Strengthen infrastructure, access to safe drinking water, hygiene and sanitation

Activity Domains

• Crop production - Water and soil conservation - Fertilization management - Water management and irrigation - Management of the cultivation system and the cultivation calendar - Training and consulting • Production animal - Management of forage resources - Hay and fodder cultivation; - Fattening and milk production. - Aviculture - Veterinary health monitoring • Exploitation of forest resources - Plantation management - Planting/reforestation - Forest protection and bushfire control

Activity domain 2

Community health and well-being

- Reproductive health - Health nutrition - Assistance for non-food items

Activity domain 3

About OREC-SAHEL

Non-Profit Organization

Mission

OREC-SAHEL's mission is to strengthen the community resilience of the highly vulnerable local population victims of insecurity or drought in order to contribute to improving access to basic social services (health, food and education).

Vision

OREC-SAHEL aspires to Niger by 2060 : A community where real food self-sufficiency is maintained; A community where more than 60% of the population has access to quality education and health; A community of peace and social cohesion.

Principle

Be accountable and transparent for one's actions because one is committed to justice; Be available at any time and place; The participation of all members in decision-making and business planning; Transparency in the management of assets, financial, material and human resources and in the management of information; Accountability or accountability for the organization's leaders to be accountable for everything they do to members; Equity in access to resources, services and information; Inclusion of all members in management and decision-making.
